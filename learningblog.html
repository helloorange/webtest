Today, I made this blog thing. I was having trouble with flexbox grid at first. I tried to style the layout using percentages, but then I realized that wasn't quite what I wanted, especially on a smaller screen.

I'm relatively happy with how this page has been styled so far. I am curious about how other coding languages could augment this blog. I could probably also set up a wordpress if I was feeling CRAZY.

Shout out to coolors for the sweet, sweet color inspiration.

Another note: I changed border-radius to rems, not ems. That way, they are all consistent across the page, although the boxes of the content are various sizes.

I know that the indents on these paragraps are not necessarily good grid design, but I really feel the shorter columns are easier on the eyes.
